IoT Ticket

A Service business can be developed by utilizing IoT ticket. Predictive maintenance creates value for the customer.

With the IoT-Ticket platform, the user can monitor the status of the device and see the locations of the devices.
Limit values are defined for the values of the sieve scales, according to which the platform's solution monitors that the required amount of optimal product is produced. A change in the ratio between the optimal grain size and the byproduct above the limit value indicates the need to replace the jaw parts.
The aim is to use the data to predict the life cycle length of the wear part. IoT-Ticket works as a customer service for utilizing data.
