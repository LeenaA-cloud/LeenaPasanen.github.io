Excel

Excel is a powerful and versatile tool that I have used a lot.
Here are some of the works I have done with Excel.

User interface plan: Sales tool for generating product price and delivery time forecasts. It is possible to form an estimate with three different scenarios. Margin calculation is used to evaluate the selling price, the delivery time evaluation is based on the previous delivery times of the products.

Pivot Tables: Data processing by sales area. I have used the pivot table for calculating, summarizing and analyzing data. It allows you to see comparisons, models and trends in the data.

Phaku, xlookup, vlookup and lookup: Functions for combining data.
